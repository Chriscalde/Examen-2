Top Club

Cargo

This cool venue, housed under railway arches in Shoreditch, showcases an incredibly diverse range of music and club nights to a warm and friendly crowd.

Top Pub

The Toucan

Pop your head into The Toucan just off Soho Square for an experience that can only be described as “Guinness galore.” Lean against walls covered in Guinness posters overlooked by a trio of the busy tiny Irish pub’s namesake toucans, drink in hand, or perch on a stool designed to look like a pint of the black stuff.

Top Restaurant

Pétrus

Enjoy Michelin star dining in London at Pétrus, the warm and stylish restaurant in the heart of Knightsbridge. Cross the Pétrus threshold and you’ll be captivated by the exquisite decor while your senses are awoken by the modern interpretations on classic flavour found throughout the irresistible menu.

Top Shopping Destinations

Oxford Street

The heart of London shopping, bustling Oxford Street has more than 300 shops, designer outlets and landmark stores: here you can find some of the best shopping in London.

Top London Tour

Total London Tour

Take a Total London tour to see sights both old and new including Westminster Abbey, the Tower of London and Buckingham Palace. See astonishing views from the Coca-Cola London Eye, and there's even a pub lunch thrown in. Chips and mushy peas anyone?
